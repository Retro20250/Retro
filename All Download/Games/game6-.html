The game is inspired by the classic Boulder Dash and features a character named Jim — a small alien ghost who explores caves on his home planet, Akturian, in search of diamonds. The gameplay involves collecting all diamonds in each level while avoiding falling rocks, enemies, and other traps. With a side-view perspective and increasing difficulty, the game challenges players’ reflexes and planning skills.
