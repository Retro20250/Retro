Mahjong Titans

Download View Gameplay Video
  • Developer(s):Oberon Games.

  • Platform(s):Windows Vista, Windows 7.
  • Release:2006.
  • Mode(s):> Single-player.

A classic puzzle game inspired by traditional Chinese Mahjong. Your goal is to match identical tiles and clear the whole board. You can only select a tile if it’s free on at least one side, it helps in Focus, quick observation, and planning.
