Icy Tower is a freeware platform game where the player controls a character named Harold the Homeboy, aiming to climb as high as possible in a tower by jumping from floor to floor. The game increases in difficulty as the player ascends, with the tower scrolling faster and requiring precise timing and combos to achieve higher scores.
