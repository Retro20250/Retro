🧠 Do You Know?

  • 🚪 His lab is bigger than his entire house, filled with lasers, robots, and gadgets—yet his parents never notice! Classic cartoon logic.
  • 👧 Dee Dee wasn’t just comic relief—she was based on Genndy Tartakovsky’s (the creator) real-life annoying sibling! Yep, that chaos is personal
  • 🎨 The show’s art style was inspired by old 1960s animation like The Jetsons and Hanna-Barbera classics—simple, bold, and colorful!
  • 🛠️ Dexter’s voice was originally done by a woman—actress Christine Cavanaugh, who also voiced Chuckie from Rugrats and Babe the pig!
  • 🧪 Dexter’s famous line “Dee Dee, get out of my laboratory!” became so iconic, it was even quoted in memes, TikToks, and pop culture references for years!
