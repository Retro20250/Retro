🧠 Do You Know?

  • 💍 Fred and Daphne were hinted to have a little romance in many versions of the show, though it was never officially confirmed in the classic series!
  • 🚐 The gang’s iconic van, the Mystery Machine, was inspired by 1960s flower-power culture—peace, love, and spooky mysteries!
  • 🍔 Shaggy and Scooby’s love for food wasn’t just random—it was meant to make them relatable as scaredy-cats who’d do anything for a snack!
  • 📜 Originally, Scooby-Doo was going to be called “Mysteries Five”, and Scooby was just the band’s mascot, not the star!
  • 🌟 Scooby-Doo’s success helped inspire other shows like The Flintstones, Josie and the Pussycats, and even Teen Titans Go!
